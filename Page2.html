Key Ingredients in Chocolate:

  • Cocoa solid:
    • Provides the chocolate flavor and color.
  • Cocoa Butter:
    • The fat extracted from cocoa beans,giving chocolate it's smooth and creamy texture.
  • Sugar:
    • Adds sweetness.
  • Milk Powder:(in milk chocolate):
    • Contributes to a creamier and lighter taste.
  • Lecithin:
    • An emulsifier(often soy or sunflower Lecithin)that helps blend the Ingredients smoothly.
  • Vanilla or other flavoring:
    • Enchances the flavor profile.

Dark ChoColate will have a higher proportion of cocoa solids and less (or no)milk, while milk chocolate has more milk and sugar. White chocolate only contains cocoa.
