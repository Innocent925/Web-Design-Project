Chocolate is a food made from roasted and ground cocoa beans that can be a liquid,solid or paste either on its own or as flavoring in other foods.

The cocoa tree has been used as a source of food for at leastg 5,300 years, starting with Mayo-Chinchipe culture in what is present day Ecuador.

Later,Mesoamerican civilizations consumed cocoa beverages,of which one,chocolate,was introduced to Europe in the 16th century.
